Dr. Stephanie R. Carter is a licensed psychologist who has been serving a multi-cultural clientele for over 30 years.

She offers individual, couple, family and group therapy, focusing on anxiety, depression, stress, relationship issues and challenging life transitions.

Dr. Carter's interest in psychology began during high school, after which she attended Arcadia University majoring in psychology. She received a Masters Degree in Education for Emotionally Disturbed Children at the University of Virgina, and worked in the Virgina school system with children and families for 5 years. She then moved to Miami where she recieved her Ph.D. in Psychology from the University of Miami.

Dr. Carter works with adolescents and adults, and enjoys parent consultations to help parents understand and respond to their children as they manage difficult problems such as divorce, behavioral problems at home or school, or sleep problems.

Dr. Carter is a member of the Florida Psychological Association (FPA) and Co-Chair of an independent group, the 'Professional Women's Healers'. Dr. Carter is also the author and narrator of The Guided Imagery Collection, and Taking Charge of Fighting Cancer.
